## Decisions Made

- Production is rolls-based end-to-end; sqft is computed on-the-fly at confirm time: `Math.round(rolls × (width_in/12) × length_ft)`. Never stored redundantly.
- WO lines can be partially scheduled across multiple runs.
- The SO-based production infrastructure is fully replaced — no backwards compatibility shim.
- Matrix and calendar unscheduled rows now show WO lines (not SO lines).
- Calendar date page schedules individual WO line runs (not groups); group scheduling remains a WO-level action on `/wo/[id]/schedule`.

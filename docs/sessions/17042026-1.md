## Decisions Made

- Vendor names use full form ("Johns Manville") throughout — CSV import uses exact match
- CSV import: duplicate PO shows diff + per-PO accept/reject (full update or full skip, no line-level)
- CSV import: changed POs replace all OPEN lines on update (delete + re-insert)
- Receiving list: split queries (two separate full queries) rather than base-string concatenation
- Early-received PO event_date in history: `LEAST(po.expected_date, CURDATE())` — pins to today
  since no `received_at` column exists on `purchase_orders`
- Receive date on PO detail: derived from `MIN(inventory_transactions.created_at)` for RECEIPT txns
